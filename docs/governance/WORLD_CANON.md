# NIN9ONE WORLD CANON

## Canon Authority

WORLD_CANON.md is the only authoritative source for confirmed world lore.
Ideas, explorations, mockups, conversations, prototype content, and draft mythology are not considered canon until recorded here.

## Confirmed Mythos

### Core Theme

The NIN9ONE World explores self-discovery through journey.

Across worlds, districts, characters, and stories, individuals often seek externally what ultimately exists within themselves.

The journey is not merely physical.

It is transformational.

### The Nature of Gold

Gold is not primarily wealth or currency.

Gold represents:

- Potential
- Truth
- Discovery
- Realization

Gold exists within the fabric of the world.

The world reveals gold.

The gold did not create the world.

### The Nature of Obsidian

Obsidian represents:

- The unknown
- Doubt
- Limitation
- The void

Obsidian is not inherently evil.

It is a necessary counterpart to discovery.

Without darkness there can be no revelation.

### Revelation Through Rupture

A recurring principle of the NIN9ONE World:

Truth is often revealed through fracture.

Cracks, breaks, and ruptures symbolize discovery rather than destruction.

What appears broken may reveal hidden value.

### The Journey

Many stories within the NIN9ONE World involve pursuit, exploration, and transformation.

The destination is rarely the true objective.

The understanding gained through the journey is the greater reward.

## Confirmed Worlds

### Nexus IV

Status: Canonical

The current primary world of exploration.

Nexus IV is a physical world.

Symbolically, it represents self-discovery and the search for internal value.

The name Nexus IV remains canonical until formally replaced.

### Queen Nefera Sol

Status: Canonical

Known as:

"The Sun Mother"

Queen Nefera Sol is the ruling sovereign of the Solari.

Queen Nefera Sol represents the highest known form of enlightenment currently established within Nexus IV.

## Confirmed Locations

### The Citadel

Status: Canonical

The Citadel serves as a central place of convergence, revelation, and understanding.

It is one of the most important locations currently established within Nexus IV.

The Citadel functions simultaneously as:

- A physical destination at the center of Nexus IV.
- A symbolic destination representing realization and understanding.

Travelers seek the city.

Seekers understand its meaning.

Detailed history remains under exploration.

## Archetypes and Transformation

The foundational archetypes of Nexus IV describe the roles characters may embody.

They do not define individual characters.

The central transformational thesis of Nexus IV is:

The journey is not about external validation.

The journey is about who you become while pursuing it.

### Traveler

Status: Canonical

A Traveler is an individual pursuing the promise of discovery.

Characteristics:

- Seeks treasure
- Seeks answers
- Seeks purpose
- Believes value lies ahead

Relationship to The Citadel:

The Traveler sees The Citadel as a destination.

Symbolic function:

The Traveler represents pursuit.

### Seeker

Status: Canonical

A Seeker is a Traveler who begins to understand that the journey itself is transformative.

Characteristics:

- Questions assumptions
- Looks beyond appearances
- Pursues meaning rather than reward

Relationship to Gold:

The Seeker understands that gold is symbolic rather than merely material.

Relationship to The Citadel:

The Seeker begins to understand what The Citadel represents.

Symbolic function:

The Seeker represents awakening.

### Sovereign

Status: Canonical

A Sovereign is an individual who has achieved alignment between purpose, identity, and understanding.

Sovereignty is not inherited.

Sovereignty is realized.

Characteristics:

- Clarity
- Wisdom
- Stewardship
- Responsibility

Relationship to Gold:

The Sovereign understands the true nature of gold.

Relationship to The Citadel:

The Sovereign understands the purpose of the destination.

Symbolic function:

The Sovereign represents realization.

### Sun Mother

Status: Canonical

The Sun Mother is the highest known embodiment of realization and ancestral understanding.

Current bearer:

Queen Nefera Sol

Characteristics:

- Wisdom
- Stewardship
- Memory
- Guidance

Symbolic function:

The Sun Mother represents enlightenment.

### Wraith

Status: Canonical

Wraiths are ancient preservers of order that predate modern civilization.

Characteristics:

- Primordial
- Enduring
- Independent
- Selective

The Wraiths are not controlled.

They choose.

Symbolic function:

Wraiths represent continuity, memory, and balance.

Open question:

The precise origin of the Wraiths remains unresolved.

### Transformation Path

The current symbolic path of transformation is:

```txt
Traveler
    ↓
Seeker
    ↓
Realization
    ↓
Sovereign
```

The Sun Mother represents the highest known realization of this journey.

## Material Ontology

The NIN9ONE World is built from foundational substances.

A material describes appearance.

A substance describes existence.

Major districts, civilizations, creatures, architecture, clothing, artifacts, and environments should inherit from or reinterpret these substances rather than replace the ontology without founder approval.

### Canonical Substances

#### Obsidian Matter

Status: Canonical

- Dark
- Dense
- Enduring
- Ancient

Symbolic meaning:

- The unknown
- Doubt
- Limitation
- The void

Manifestations:

- Architecture
- Terrain
- Monuments
- Weapons
- Armor

World rule:

Obsidian is not evil.

It is the necessary counterpart to discovery.

#### Solar Gold

The world reveals gold.

### Substance Relationships

The current symbolic relationship model is:

- Obsidian Matter leads to World.
- Solar Gold leads to Revelation.
- Glass leads to Perception.
- Living Filament leads to Connection.
- Atmospheric Matter leads to Mystery.

These relationships are symbolic and may evolve as mythology develops.

### World-Building Rules

1. All major civilizations should express one or more foundational substances.
2. Future worlds should reinterpret the ontology rather than replace it.
3. Visual design decisions should reinforce substance meaning.
4. New substances require founder approval before canonization.
