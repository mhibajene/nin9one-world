# NIN9ONE WORLD CANON

## Canon Authority

WORLD_CANON.md is the only authoritative source for confirmed world lore.
Ideas, explorations, mockups, conversations, prototype content, and draft mythology are not considered canon until recorded here.

## Confirmed Mythos

### Core Theme

The NIN9ONE World explores self-discovery through journey.

Across worlds, districts, characters, and stories, individuals often seek externally what ultimately exists within themselves.

The journey is not merely physical.

It is transformational.

### The Nature of Gold

Gold is not primarily wealth or currency.

Gold represents:

- Potential
- Truth
- Discovery
- Realization

Gold exists within the fabric of the world.

The world reveals gold.

The gold did not create the world.

### The Nature of Obsidian

Obsidian represents:

- The unknown
- Doubt
- Limitation
- The void

Obsidian is not inherently evil.

It is a necessary counterpart to discovery.

Without darkness there can be no revelation.

### Revelation Through Rupture

A recurring principle of the NIN9ONE World:

Truth is often revealed through fracture.

Cracks, breaks, and ruptures symbolize discovery rather than destruction.

What appears broken may reveal hidden value.

### The Journey

Many stories within the NIN9ONE World involve pursuit, exploration, and transformation.

The destination is rarely the true objective.

The understanding gained through the journey is the greater reward.

## Material Ontology

The NIN9ONE World is built from foundational substances.

A material describes appearance.

A substance describes existence.

Major districts, civilizations, creatures, architecture, clothing, artifacts, and environments should inherit from or reinterpret these substances rather than replace the ontology without founder approval.

### Canonical Substances

#### Obsidian Matter

Status: Canonical

Physical characteristics:

- Dark
- Dense
- Enduring
- Ancient

Symbolic meaning:

- The unknown
- Doubt
- Limitation
- The void

Manifestations:

- Architecture
- Terrain
- Monuments
- Weapons
- Armor

World rule:

Obsidian is not evil.

It is the necessary counterpart to discovery.

#### Solar Gold

Status: Canonical

Physical characteristics:

- Luminous
- Conductive
- Revealing

Symbolic meaning:

- Potential
- Truth
- Discovery
- Realization

Manifestations:

- Veins
- Markings
- Artifacts
- Symbols
- Sacred architecture

World rule:

The world reveals gold.

Gold did not create the world.

#### Atmospheric Matter

Status: Canonical

Physical characteristics:

- Dust
- Mist
- Storm
- Haze

Narrative purpose:

Atmospheric Matter creates mystery, scale, and transition.

Atmosphere is an active world-building substance rather than a passive visual effect.

Status: Exploratory

Physical characteristics:

- Connected

Observed in:

- Certain garments

Open questions:

### Substance Relationships

The current symbolic relationship model is:

- Obsidian Matter leads to World.
- Solar Gold leads to Revelation.
- Glass leads to Perception.
- Living Filament leads to Connection.
- Atmospheric Matter leads to Mystery.

These relationships are symbolic and may evolve as mythology develops.

### World-Building Rules

1. All major civilizations should express one or more foundational substances.
2. Future worlds should reinterpret the ontology rather than replace it.
3. Visual design decisions should reinforce substance meaning.
4. New substances require founder approval before canonization.
