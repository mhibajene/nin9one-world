# NIN9ONE WORLD CANON

## Canon Authority

WORLD_CANON.md is the only authoritative source for confirmed world lore.
Ideas, explorations, mockups, conversations, prototype content, and draft mythology are not considered canon until recorded here.

## Confirmed Mythos

### Core Theme

The NIN9ONE World explores self-discovery through journey.

Across worlds, districts, characters, and stories, individuals often seek externally what ultimately exists within themselves.

The journey is not merely physical.

It is transformational.

### The Nature of Gold

Gold is not primarily wealth or currency.

Gold represents:

- Potential
- Truth
- Discovery
- Realization

Gold exists within the fabric of the world.

The world reveals gold.

The gold did not create the world.

### The Nature of Obsidian

Obsidian represents:

- The unknown
- Doubt
- Limitation
- The void

Obsidian is not inherently evil.

It is a necessary counterpart to discovery.

Without darkness there can be no revelation.

### Revelation Through Rupture

A recurring principle of the NIN9ONE World:

Truth is often revealed through fracture.

Cracks, breaks, and ruptures symbolize discovery rather than destruction.

What appears broken may reveal hidden value.

### The Journey

Many stories within the NIN9ONE World involve pursuit, exploration, and transformation.

The destination is rarely the true objective.

The understanding gained through the journey is the greater reward.

## Confirmed Worlds

### Nexus IV

Status: Canonical

The current primary world of exploration.

Nexus IV is a physical world.

Symbolically, it represents self-discovery and the search for internal value.

The name Nexus IV remains canonical until formally replaced.
